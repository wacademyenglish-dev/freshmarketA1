Grammar: Pointing at food

How does the vlogger show the food to the camera?

"This is an avocado." (1 item)

"These are grapes." (Many items)

Select the correct rule:

RULE UNLOCKED!

Correct! "This is" = Este é (Singular). "These are" = Estes são (Plural).

Point to objects near you. Say "This is a..." and "These are...".

Grammar: A or An?

Look at the vegetables. When do we use "A" and when do we use "AN"?

"This is A potato."

"This is AN eggplant."

Select the correct rule:

RULE UNLOCKED!

Correct! We say AN avocado, AN onion, AN eggplant. Everything else is A.

What about "apple"? Do you say A apple or AN apple?

LOGIC GATE 2

"This in my hands is ________ onion."

Correct! An onion. What color was the onion in the video? (It was brown).

Syntax Builder 2/2

Construct the sentence about the peppers.

Yes! Because "peppers" is plural, we use "These are". Do you like spicy food?
